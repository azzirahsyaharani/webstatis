About THV

V is a member of BTS, a South Korean boy group that recently has gone worldwide. "V" is actually his stage name, while his real one is Kim Taehyung....Born on December 30, 1995, many people idolize him because of his mesmerizing face. As if everyone was bewitched by his brown eyes and straight nose. Not to mention, V has a defined jawline making him look so manly. His height is, too, quite ideal, which is around 179 cm. No wonder he was ranked first in the "Top 100 Most Handsome Faces of 2017". However, besides being head over heels for his amazing visual, some fans are actually fond of V's dancing and singing ability. In BTS, apart from being a Visual, he is also a Lead Dancer and Vocalist
